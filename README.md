# 🚗 Car Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Car Sales Analysis Dashboard, designed to provide comprehensive insights into sales performance. By leveraging data visualization, this dashboard enables stakeholders to track revenue trends, understand customer demographics, and evaluate product performance across various categories.

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard provides real-time tracking of critical business metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Total Cars Sold
* Total Customers
* Price Analytics: Average, Highest, and Lowest car prices.

## 🔍 Key Analytical Insights

The dashboard incorporates multiple analytical perspectives to support data-driven decision-making:

* Performance Metrics: Top 10 companies by revenue and regional revenue distribution.
* Customer Segmentation: Detailed breakdown by gender.
* Product Preferences: Analysis of body styles and transmission types.
* Trend Analysis: Tracking revenue growth throughout the year (Revenue Over Time).
* Model Performance: Rankings of the most sold car models.

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques

* Power BI: Developed the interactive dashboard and visual layout.
* Data Modeling: Constructed a robust model for efficient data processing.
* Interactive Filtering: Integrated dynamic filters (Year, Company, Model, Dealer Region, Gender, Body Style, Transmission, Color) for a personalized user experience.
* DAX: Utilized advanced DAX measures to calculate performance metrics and business KPIs.
